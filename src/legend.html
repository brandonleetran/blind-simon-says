Blind Simon Says

By Brandon lee Tran

How to Play

Use the keyboard's tab keys to navigate through the game.

  1. Pay close attention to the pattern shown on the screen.
  2. Memorize the sequence of colors and sounds that Blind Simon presents to you.
  3. Once the pattern is displayed, it's your turn to repeat it back correctly.
  4. Be careful! Blind Simon won't show you the sequence again, so rely on your memory.
  5. The game will become progressively more challenging as you successfully complete each round.
Legend

Move backward: SHIFT + TAB key
