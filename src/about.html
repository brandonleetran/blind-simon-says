Blind Simon Says

About

Welcome to Blind Simon Says, the ultimate memory and pattern recognition web app game! Blind Simon Says is a modern twist on the classic Simon Says game, designed to challenge and entertain players of all ages. Get ready to test your memory, reflexes, and concentration in a fun and interactive way! This game targets screenreaders. However, may be played on non-assistive technologies as well.

Features

Engaging Gameplay: Blind Simon Says offers an immersive and addictive gaming experience that keeps you coming back for more.

Colorful and Intuitive Interface: The game boasts a visually appealing design, making it enjoyable for players of all ages.

Privacy and Security

At Blind Simon Says, we prioritize your privacy and data security. Rest assured that all your personal information is handled with the utmost care, and the game is built with the latest security measures to protect your data.

We may use cookies to store your high scores and game progress, allowing you to track your achievements and improve your performance. These cookies are only used for internal purposes within the game and are not shared with any third parties.

Give Feedback

As we are continually striving to enhance the game, your feedback is crucial to us. If you encounter any issues, have suggestions, or want to share your experience, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. Your input will shape the future of Blind Simon Says!

Join us in this exciting memory challenge and see how far you can go in the world of Blind Simon Says. Sharpen your memory, follow the patterns, and become the ultimate Simon Says champion! Thank you for being a part of this adventure.

Disclaimer

Blind Simon Says is a web app game created for entertainment and educational purposes. It is not affiliated with the original Simon Says physical game or any official Simon Says product. Enjoy the game responsibly and have fun!
